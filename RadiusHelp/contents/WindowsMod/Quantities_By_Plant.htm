Quantities By Plant

Use the Quantities By Plant window to view the plants to which the order has been allocated. 

To display this window, click  View Quantities by Plant under the Sales Order Details section of the Sales Orders page.

To search search the list, enter your search criteria in the fields at the top and the grid automatically displays the search results. 

Column 

Description

Plant

The plant where is being performed.

Required Qty

 The required quantity from that plant. 

Unplanned Qty

The quantity that has not yet been planned for production.
