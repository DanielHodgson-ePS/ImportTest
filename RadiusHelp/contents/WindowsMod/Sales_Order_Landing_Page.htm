Sales Orders (Landing Page)

Use the landing page of the Sales Order page to quickly and easily find sales order records, view data about the sales orders and create new sales orders. 

The search bar at the top of the screen is used to quickly filter which sales order records display in the sales order table. Alternatively, you can click  Advanced Search next to the search bar to access the Advanced Search Filters window, where you can leverage the Advanced Search engine to build powerful queries to filter records by (which can then be saved for quick access).

Toolbar Bar

Toolbar Bar

The toolbar at the top of the page houses the search and also displays your current company, plant, language and logged in user; which can be changed at any time.

Click to display further filter options or to create a custom view:

  •   Save View: Click to save the current filter bar selection.
