Shipment Tracking Details

Use the Shipment Tracking Details window to view all tracking numbers associated with a shipment and their individual freight value. 

To display this window, click  Shipment Tracking Details under the Shipping > Shipping Request Headers section of the Sales Orders page.

Column 

Description

Tracking Number

The tracking numbers associated with this shipment. There might be multiple shipments for the one shipping request. 

Freight Value

The value of the individual shipment.
