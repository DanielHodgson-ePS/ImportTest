Purchase Orders

Use the Purchase Orders page, accessed by clicking Purchase Order  on the navigation pane, to create new purchase orders or amend existing purchase orders which can then be sent to suppliers. The purchase order price is used by invoice checking to verify that the supplier’s purchase invoice is correctly priced.

The Purchace Orders page is composed of the following sections, which can be switched using the following buttons at the top of the page:

  • My Workspace: The workspace option allows you to create views and layouts for your purchase requirements and purchase orders. Select from one of the following options to open the corresponding segment:
    • Purchase Requirement: Click to display your purchase requirement views.
    • Purchase Order: Click to display your purchase order views.
  • Requirements: Click to open the purchase requirements browser.
  • Purchase Orders: Click to display the purchase orders browser.

My Workspace (PR) Section

The purchase requirements workspace provides a convenient location to quickly filter and create your purchase requirements. The page is divided into filtered columns, which display purchase requirements based on the search criteria of that filter. 

You can drag and drop purchase requirements into the Add to PO Line column, then select one of the following to generate an order for the requirement:

  • Generate PO > Create New PO: Opens the Generate PO window to create a new purchase order for that requirement.
  • Generate PO > Add to Exisitng PO: Opens the ... window to add the requirement to an existing purchase order.

My Workspace (PO) Section

The Purchase Orders workspace provides a convenient hub in which to view and access your purchase order records. The page is divided into filtered columns, which display purchase requirements based on the search criteria of that filter. 

By default, the following filters are provided and are based on the status of the purchase order:

  • On Hold
  • Authorized
  • Confirmed
  • Cancelled

 

 

Purchase Orders Browser Section

Use the Purchase Orders browser page to search for purchase order records. You cna click a Purchase Order Number to open that record. 

 

Purchase Requirements Browser Section

Use the Purchase Requirements browser to search for purchase requirement records. You can click a Requirement ID to open that record.
