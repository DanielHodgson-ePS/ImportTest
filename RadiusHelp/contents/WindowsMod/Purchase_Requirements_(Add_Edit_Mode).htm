Purchase Requirements (Add/Edit Mode)

Use the Purchase Requirements (Add/Edit) window to purchase items and materials by the requirements of a job or estimate.

Requirement Details

The Requirement Details section contains the main details of the purchase requirement record; holding the item, status, required quantity, job and other details.

Annotations Section

 

Inventory Tab

The Inventory tab allows you to check whether the requirement can be fulfilled from inventory.

If there is free stock that you want to use to satisfy all or part of the requirement, click on the stock line to be reserved in the Available table, and then click to move it the Reserved table. Enter the quantity to be reserved in the Reserved Quantity column of the Reserved table,then click  to save your reservation.

The requirement status is updated to In Inventory if the full requirement quantity has been satisfied.  Otherwise, the status is left as Unplanned; signifying that further action is required to satisfy the full requirement.

 

PO Tab

The PO tab displays any undelivered purchase order lines for this item that have free amounts. If you decide to satisfy all or part of the requirement from one of these lines, click on the order line to be reserved in the Available table, and then click to move it the Reserved table. Enter the quantity to be reserved in the Reserved Quantity column of the Reserved table,then click  to save your reservation.

The requirement status is updated to In Inventory if the full requirement quantity has been satisfied.  Otherwise, the status is left as Unplanned; signifying that further action is required to satisfy the full requirement.

Jobs Tab

The Reserved Jobs folder displays details of all reservations made against a Production Job for this requirement. If you decide to satisfy all or part of the requirement from one of these lines, click on the order line to be reserved in the Available table, and then click to move it the Reserved table. Enter the quantity to be reserved in the Reserved Quantity column of the Reserved table,then click  to save your reservation.

The requirement status is updated to In Inventory if the full requirement quantity has been satisfied.  Otherwise, the status is left as Unplanned; signifying that further action is required to satisfy the full requirement.

Balances Section

The balances sections displays a visual representation of your item balances. Youn can select whether to view your data as a chart or a table by clicking either in Chart View  or Table View , respectively.

You can choose to view balances for the following by selecting the corresponding option from the dropdown field:

  • Quantities
  • Values
  • References
  • Free Stock
